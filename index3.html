Family is the strongest relationship that each and every one has. For a teenager like me,
the world is full of experiences. As I mature, my relationship with my family, touch all
areas of my development - thinking, social, emotional, physical, behavioural, and
moral. We have a stable commitment that helps me a lot to develop good self-
confidence, motivation to learn and achievement in school. Later in my life, I could say
that they are the best instrument to me as I walk into the next chapter of my life journey.

We strengthen our relationship by finding a time every day to be together as a family.
For us, it is the most important way to give each and every one the chance to talk
about things happened in a day. Even if my elder sister is currently live in the UAE for her
work, we adjust our time to talk with her using Skype or sometimes just only Facebook.
With that, distance doesn't matter when it comes to our family. It is a matter of giving
our love, trust, and support to each and every one of us.
