It's good for your happiness to be a great friend to someone and to have a group of
good friends supporting you. Friends will come and go in your life, but more important
than how long a friendship lasts, is that a good friend will love you for who you are.

I've found my true real friends back when I was in High school. We started to have a
group called "Bekis" which has a funny story behind that name tho, LOL. We called
each other "beki" (beki means gay) when one of my friend started to call each one of
us "beki" with a funny tone of voice. With that, we started also to call everyone in the
group BEKI! I love them a lot, even though some of them are not straight. Yes, true
beki! But still, they're perfect for being my true bestfriends!

When we entered college, we met another set of friends but still, our friendship stays
the same as it is. I met a bunch of people in college but I could say that I only have two
true friends remain out of them. I don't regret having two real friends in college
because it's absolutely hard to find real people in college and I'm super happy that I
found two. At least, they're real.
